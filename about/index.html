Our Journey

Uniting Faith to End Homelessness

Our goal is to bring churches and faith-based organizations into shared learning, work, and advocacy — guided by best practices to END homelessness in America.

Born from the Need for Dignity and Belonging

The Coalition to End Homelessness was born from the need for dignity and belonging in our communities. Together, we strive to bring hope and resources to those in need.

We are the connective tissue — a shared learning community, a coordinating body, and an advocacy platform that helps faith communities move from isolated compassion to collective, lasting impact.

Join Us

Empowering Communities Together

Our mission is to unite faith-based organizations to tackle homelessness through collective action and advocacy. Together, we will create a world where everyone has a place to call home.

We share what works. We build what’s missing. We help churches move from feeding the hungry one at a time to helping end the systems that create hunger in the first place.
