Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 10, 2026

The National Faith Coalition to End Homelessness is committed to making nfceh.org accessible to people with disabilities. Radical welcome is one of our core principles — and that includes how this website is built.

Conformance Target

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA.

Measures We Take

  • Semantic HTML with a logical heading structure on every page
  • Color combinations chosen and checked to meet contrast requirements
  • Full keyboard navigability, including a skip-to-content link and keyboard-accessible menus
  • Alternative text on meaningful images, with decorative images hidden from assistive technology
  • Readable typography and a layout that adapts to screen size and zoom
  • Minimal use of motion and no auto-playing media

Third-Party Content

Some functions of our site link to services we do not control, including Mailchimp (newsletter signup and registration), Pushpay (donations), and Printful (store). We cannot guarantee the accessibility of those pages and declare partial conformance for journeys that pass through them.

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier on this site, we genuinely want to know. Email info@nfceh.org and we will do our best to fix the issue or provide the information you need in another format.

Our Mission

Our goal is to bring churches and faith-based organizations into shared learning, work, and advocacy — guided by best practices to END homelessness in America.
