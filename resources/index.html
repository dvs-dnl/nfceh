Find the work already happening

Every region of the country has a homelessness response system, coordinated through a local Continuum of Care. These networks already know who is experiencing homelessness in your community and where the gaps are. Connecting your congregation to them is one of the most effective steps you can take — and we can help you make that warm introduction.
